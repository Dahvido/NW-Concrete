About Us

NW Concreteworks, Inc. began in early 2006. We love building with CONCRETE. It is our desire to provide our regional designers, builders/contractors and communities with a positive and professional service in our trade. MONTANA is our home, and we are very familiar with the challenges of working with this dynamic material in its various applications

What We Do

Footings and Foundations of all shapes and sizes
ICF wall construction (insulated concrete form)
Slab on Grade and Slab on Deck
Civil Concrete – curbs, sidewalks and steps of all configurations
Architectural Hardscapes – colored and stamped concrete, decorative overlays and refurbishing concrete floors
